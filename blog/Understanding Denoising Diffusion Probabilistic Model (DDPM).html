Introduction

Currently, everyone hears about diffusion models that generate image via "denoising". However, as some important details are not presented in the original paper DDPM[2], it is difficult for newcomers to understand the derivation of the final simplified objective used in the original DDPM paper. Therefore, this blog will cover the "corners" that are seldomly mentioned elsewhere. We will start from deriving the simplified objective in \ref{eq:lsimple}, explaining why we drop some terms from the objective or make unintuitive simplifications.

It's assumed that audiences understand the basics of DDPM[2], such as the definition of forward and reverse processes, the derivation of final simplified objective. Let's review the derivation of the Eq.\ref{eq:lsimple} in details.
