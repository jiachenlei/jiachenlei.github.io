Understanding Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Model (DDPM)

Abstract

This blog covered the following topics: (working in progress)
- Introduced technical details of DDPM including step-by-step mathematical derivations.
- Introduced DDIM, Denoising diffusion implicit model, which is proposed for sampling acceleration.
- Draw connections between DDPM and current generative models.

A highly recommended blog is [1], where most details on DDPM, DDIM, LDM, guidance, and current large-scale DMs, etc, are presented. In this blog, I mainly focus on details that are not mentioned in [1] but are somewhat confusing. Therefore, it's assumed that audiences understand how do DDPMs work and know the basic formulas in DDPM[2].
